Margherita Pizza

Home

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 15 min

Cook Time: 15 min

Total Time: 30 min + time to make pizza dough and let it rise

Ingredients

  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • Flour (for stretching the dough)
  • 2 homemade pizza doughs
  • 200 g fresh mozzarella
  • 50 g Parmigiano-Reggiano
  • 15 g chopped fresh basil
  • 1 tbsp cornmeal (for bakin)

Instructions

  1. Make the marinara souce: In a medium bowl, mix tomatoes, garlic, salt, sugar, pepper, and oil.
  2. Preheat the oven to 250 ℃ and set an oven rack a in the bottom position. Dust a baking sheet with half the cornmeal.
  3. On a floured surface, stretch the dough (30 cm diameter) and transfer to the baking sheet.
  4. Spead the sauce over the dough, leaving a border around the edges. Bake for 7 minutes, until the crust is partially cooked. Take out and spread half of the mozzarella and Parmigiano-Reggiano. Put back into the oven and cook until the crust is finished and cheese is melted (about 4 minutes). Remove the pizza from the oven, sprinkle with basil, and slice. Repeat with remaining dough and ingredients.

Recipe source: Margherita Pizza by Jennifer Segal
