Hamburger

Home

Servings: 4

Prep Time: 10 min

Cook Time: 10 min

Total Time: 20 min

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 pounds lean ground beef
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 1/2 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 ounce dy onion soup mix
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp dried parsley
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/2 tsp crushed dried rismary
  • pinch of salt and pepper or to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, cheese, egg, onion soup mix, minced garlic, garlic powder, soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, parsley, basil, oregano, rosemary, salt, and pepper in a large bowl.
  3. Mix and form 4 patties.
  4. Cook patties on grill until no linger pink in the ceter and juices run clear (4-5 minutes per side). Inner temperature should read at least 74 ℃
