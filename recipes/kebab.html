Kebab

Home

Servings: 4 to 6

Prep Time: 15 min

Cook Time: 22 min

Total Time: 37 min

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil, divided
  • 1 small yello onion, grated large
  • 1/2 red bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 2 pounds ground beef
  • 1/3 cup finely chopped parsley
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp ground cayenne
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 425 Farenheit.
  2. Drain onion and bell pepper.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and mix.
  4. Spread mixture onto a baking sheet and use a knife to cut strips.
  5. Bake the kebabs for roughtly 20 to 22 minutes.
  6. Broil to finish. Separate and serve.

Recipe source: These Super Easy Sheet Pan Kebabs Skip the Skewers by Shayma Saadat
